# Project 20 – Enterprise Digital Twin Graph

Enterprise Digital Twin Platform using Neo4j for Business Process Mapping, Enterprise Architecture, Operational Intelligence, Real-Time Analytics, and Executive Decision Support.

---

# Overview

This project demonstrates Enterprise Digital Twin architecture.

The platform provides:

- Enterprise Process Modeling
- Business Capability Mapping
- Operational Intelligence
- Real-Time Monitoring
- Dependency Analysis
- Executive Decision Intelligence

---

# Business Scenario

Large organizations operate across:

- Business Units
- Enterprise Systems
- Business Processes
- Operational Workflows
- Technology Platforms
- Strategic Initiatives

Understanding how all enterprise components interact is essential for successful digital transformation.

Digital Twin technology creates a virtual representation of the enterprise.

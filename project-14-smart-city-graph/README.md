# Project 14 – Smart City Graph

Enterprise Smart City Graph Platform using Neo4j for transportation analytics, utility management, IoT intelligence, and urban digital transformation.

---

# Overview

This project demonstrates graph-powered smart city intelligence.

The platform provides:

- Transportation Analytics
- Utility Intelligence
- IoT Device Management
- Smart City Services
- Urban Analytics
- Digital Twin Visualization

---

# Business Scenario

Modern smart cities manage:

- Citizens
- Transportation Systems
- Utility Networks
- IoT Devices
- Public Services
- Infrastructure Assets

Graph databases help city authorities understand relationships between infrastructure, services, and citizens.

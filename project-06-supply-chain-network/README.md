# Project 06 – Supply Chain Network

Enterprise Supply Chain Network Platform using Neo4j for supplier intelligence, logistics optimization, risk analysis, and distribution network visibility.

---

# Overview

This project demonstrates graph-based supply chain analytics.

The platform provides:

- Supplier Relationship Management
- Logistics Network Analysis
- Distribution Intelligence
- Supply Chain Risk Analysis
- Route Optimization
- Vendor Performance Monitoring

---

# Business Scenario

Large organizations operate complex supply chains involving:

- Suppliers
- Manufacturers
- Warehouses
- Distribution Centers
- Transportation Networks
- Retail Locations

Traditional systems struggle to visualize supply chain dependencies.

Graph databases provide end-to-end visibility and intelligence.

# Project 13 – Enterprise Asset Graph

Enterprise Asset Graph Platform using Neo4j for asset lifecycle management, maintenance intelligence, dependency analysis, and enterprise operations visibility.

---

# Overview

This project demonstrates graph-powered enterprise asset management.

The platform provides:

- Asset Lifecycle Management
- Maintenance Intelligence
- Asset Dependency Analysis
- Facility Management
- Asset Risk Assessment
- Operational Analytics

---

# Business Scenario

Large enterprises manage thousands of assets across:

- Buildings
- Equipment
- Vehicles
- IT Infrastructure
- Manufacturing Assets
- Operational Facilities

Understanding asset relationships is critical for maintenance, risk management, and operational efficiency.

Graph databases provide visibility into asset dependencies and lifecycle performance.

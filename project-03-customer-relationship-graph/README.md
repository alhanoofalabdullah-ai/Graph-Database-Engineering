# Project 03 – Customer Relationship Graph

Enterprise Customer Relationship Graph using Neo4j for Customer 360, customer journey analysis, loyalty analytics, and relationship intelligence.

---

# Overview

This project demonstrates graph-based customer intelligence.

The platform provides:

- Customer 360 View
- Customer Journey Analysis
- Relationship Intelligence
- Customer Segmentation
- Loyalty Analytics
- Customer Value Analysis

---

# Business Scenario

Organizations collect customer information from:

- CRM Systems
- Sales Platforms
- Marketing Systems
- Loyalty Programs
- Customer Service Platforms

Customer data is often fragmented across systems.

Graph databases connect customer relationships into a unified Customer 360 view.
